Karate Kids
UX/UI ReDesign

Karate Kids is a karate school that provides different level of karate classes, while encouraging the next generation to be active, peaceful and considerate people.

  • Group project
  • Brand look/feel
  • Booking UX flows
  • Responsive web design

Define

During our research we found that users become stressed when trying to organise after school activities. When looking at a website parents expect clear navigation and easy access to information. We also found that users are more willing to trust a website that looks professional and up to date. How might we increase the motivation for children to join karate, while implementing a smooth booking online system for busy parents?

Ideate

On the original website, users are likely to feel lost looking for a timetable to book in classes and spends considerate amount of time scrolling through text, they also struggle to find the information they need. From the Heuristic Evaluation, we concluded that we need an overall brand update and site navigation cleanup. Our main task is to have a simple and intuitive online class booking process in place.

Design

To ensure that Karate Kids is consistently presented as a energetic brand, a UI Style Guide was meticulously developed. This guide outlines the design principles and visual elements that embody Karate Kids, such as its iconic logo, a color scheme that demonstrates different belt classes, and the typography that is legible and professional.

Prototype

After several iterations of wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes, we have arrived at our polished high-fidelity prototype.
